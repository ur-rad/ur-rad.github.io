Program

Event Details

Date: November 6-8, 2025

Location: Westin Arlington Gateway, Arlington, VA, USA

Event: AAAI Fall Symposium

Format: In-person

Symposium Format

The symposium will be made up of paper presentations, keynote talks, panels, and breakout discussions.

Paper Presentations

Accepted papers will be presented during dedicated sessions, allowing authors to share their research and engage with the community.

Keynote Talks

Distinguished speakers from academia and industry will present their insights on robot application development.

Panel Discussions

Interactive panels will explore key challenges and opportunities in unifying representations for robotics.

Breakout Sessions

Focused discussions on specific topics will encourage collaboration and networking among participants.
