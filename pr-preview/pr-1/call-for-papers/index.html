Call for Papers

We invite submissions for the 3rd AAAI Fall Symposium on Unifying Representations for Robot Application Development.

Ready to Submit?

Submit Your Paper

Submissions open now • Preferred deadline: August 7, 2025

Topics of Interest

Research areas we welcome for submission

  • Representational trends
  • Representations for robot learning
  • AI planning for robotics
  • Formal methods in robotics
  • Natural language as a representation
  • Novel representations & novel representation uses
  • Representations for user interfaces
  • Robot end-user development
  • Robot programming interfaces & paradigms
  • Robot runtime/control environments
  • Opportunities for standardization
  • Frameworks (e.g., ROS or middleware)
  • Open-source & collaboration initiatives
  • Identifying representation requirements

Submission Types

Choose the format that best fits your contribution

We invite the following contributions, formatted using the AAAI-25 author kit:

Full Papers

4-8 pages
archival OR non-archival

For novel research, artifact submissions, or strong works in progress.

Short Papers

2-4 pages
archival OR non-archival

For positions, smaller artifacts, and early work in progress.

Abstracts

1 page
non-archival only

For sharing ideas.

Important Dates & Timeline

Key deadlines and submission process

Before Submission (Optional)

Help us plan for reviewers by emailing urrad.symposium@gmail.com with:

  • Subject: "[UR-RAD] Intent to Submit"
  • Tentative title, author list, and keywords

Note: At least one author from each paper will be expected to review 2-3 other UR-RAD papers.

  • Round 2 Short Abstracts Due - Required for late submissions
